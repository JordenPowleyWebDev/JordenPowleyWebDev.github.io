About
Jorden Powley

I am a web developer based around Derby

After graduating Keele University in 2016 with a Bachelor's Degree in BSc Computer Science, I spent two years working form Zonal Marketing technologies (formally TXD Limited). Here I worked on many projects ranging from building marketing email templates to gift card e-commerce systems, for clients include New World Trading Company, Butcombe Brewery and All Star Lanes.

Following my departure from ZMT I worked for The Together Agency. A creative branding agency based in Nottingham. Here I primarily migrated websites and applications to use a Bitbucket pipeline / AWS architecture with a smattering of smaller projects along the line.

From October the 21st 2019 I will be working for Langley Foxall on what I believe will be some exciting new projects!

Occasionally in my spare time I do smaller development projects for myself, however like the typical developer these are usually far to grand a scale and get put down as soon as I get my next big idea...

Doesn't work 100% of the time

Being an ever evolving individual, verbose way of saying 'short attention span', my hobbies reflect this.

Currently these include kayaking, running, mediocre djing and repairing my (inflatable) kayak.
